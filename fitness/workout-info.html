Workout plan and information

Full Body Strength Training

  • Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light cardio (e.g., jogging, jumping jacks, or cycling)
  • Squats: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Dumbbell Rows: 3 sets of 10-12 reps (each arm)
  • Lunges: 3 sets of 10-12 reps (each leg)
  • Plank: 3 sets, hold for 30-60 seconds
  • Cool down: Stretching for major muscle groups (hamstrings, quads, chest, back, shoulders)

Cardio and Core

  • Warm-up: 5-10 minutes of light cardio (e.g., brisk walking, jogging)
  • Cardio Interval Training: 20 minutes (alternate between high-intensity intervals and moderate intensity)
  • Crunches: 3 sets of 15-20 reps
  • Russian Twists: 3 sets of 15-20 reps (with or without weight)
  • Bicycle Crunches: 3 sets of 15-20 reps (each side)
  • Plank with Leg Lift: 3 sets, hold for 30-60 seconds (each side)
  • Cool down: Stretching for core muscles (obliques, abs, lower back)

Rest or Active Recovery

Rest day or engage in light activities like walking, yoga, or swimming to promote recovery.
