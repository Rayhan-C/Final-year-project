Healthy Recipes & Nutrition

Searching for a few fresh, healthful recipes to incorporate into your weekly menu?

Get Started

BREAKFAST: AVOCADO TOAST WITH POACHED EGG

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of whole grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: cherry tomatoes, sliced radishes, microgreens

LUNCH: CAPRESE QUINOA SALAD

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa, cooled
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup fresh mozzarella balls, halved
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, torn
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, cherry tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and torn basil leaves.
  2. Drizzle the extra virgin olive oil and balsamic vinegar over the salad.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Toss gently to combine all the ingredients.
  5. Serve immediately or refrigerate until ready to eat.

AFTERNOON SNACK: GREEK YOGURT WITH HONEY AND ALMONDS

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 2 tablespoons sliced almonds
Instructions:
  1. In a bowl, scoop the Greek yogurt.
  2. Drizzle honey over the yogurt.
  3. Sprinkle sliced almonds on top.
  4. Enjoy as a refreshing and protein-rich snack.

DINNER: VEGETABLE STIR-FRY WITH TOFU

Ingredients:

  • 200g firm tofu, cubed
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (such as bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, snap peas)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • Cooked brown rice or quinoa for serving
Instructions:
  1. Heat sesame oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and cubed tofu to the skillet, stirring occasionally until tofu is golden brown.
  3. Add mixed vegetables to the skillet and cook until they are tender-crisp.
  4. Pour soy sauce over the tofu and vegetables, stirring to combine.
  5. Continue cooking for another 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  6. Serve the vegetable stir-fry over cooked brown rice or quinoa.
